    Message From Founder Chairman



    It's been many decades since our family rooted the flower of social work , we have been the working hands and witness of this work for social welfare. we established this school to give a basic primary education ,which is so important to the ones whom it is not easily accesible. The BDM school is doing a great work in the field of primary education .
    Bishambher Dutt Memorial School at Gular Dogi, Tehri Garhwal is the culmination of the vision of my father. It is a very pleasing experience to see a school which is able to give its students an education coated with affinity. This is what makes BDm different from other institutions.

    My best wishes to all the stake holders and our sanguine that school shall continue playing a significant role for the betterment of the society. With best wishes...

    Founder And Chairman
    Vikas Chandra Rayal

       About Founder 

    Shri. Vikash chandra Rayal has lost his father at the age of 11. After that he and his family has to face many financial problems. Sri Vikash chandra Rayal has three sister's and one brother. Farming and small pension was the only way to take care of children to his mother. His mother did much struggle to educate them. They did not get proper guidance.Shri Vikash chandra Rayal studied in government hindi medium school. School was very far from villages to every student. His family faced many other problem But vikash chandra Rayal was very bright and intelligent student and got 75.6 % in 10th board and toped in area also get 62% in 12th first division. after 12th he did job in shops to earn money so that he continued his education and could take admission in college. He was in BSc but during BSc he took admission in BTech and while his course he started giving tutions or coaching to students upto class 12th and become very famous teacher of physics in local area. during his study he took a decision to educate his village children and to give them a better and quality education so that they can stand anywhere and they can have a quality education just like in cities in less fee. And if anyone cannot afford it would be free.
